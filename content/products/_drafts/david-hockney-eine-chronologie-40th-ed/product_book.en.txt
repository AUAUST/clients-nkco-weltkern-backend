  <p>Pop artist, painter of modern life, landscape painter, color virtuoso, explorer of image and perception &#8211; for six decades David Hockney has been known as an artist who always finds new ways to explore the world and its possibilities of representation. He consistently creates unforgettable images: free works with drawn lines and integrated text from the Swinging London period of the 1960s; the famous Swimming Pool series chronicling the Californian way of life in the 1970&#8217;s; meticulously observed portraits and brightly colored oversized landscapes after his late return to his native Yorkshire.
