description: '<p>A collectible volume for graphic designers and creative entrepreneurs, this book published by Maximage is dedicated to <em>Color Library</em>, one of the most discussed projects in the field of graphic design in recent years. An investigation into the reproduction and printing of colors, <em>Color Library</em> is a tool for artists, designers, photographers and printers. It aims to expand the possibilities of color printing and reflects our contemporary perception of color as shaped by recent developments in print production. <em>Color Library</em> was created as part of the Workflow research project (Ra&#038;D) conducted at ECAL on printing technologies. A project led by Maximage and supported by the HES-SO.</p>'
