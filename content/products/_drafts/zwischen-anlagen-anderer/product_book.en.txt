description: |
  <p>The value of the property must increase! This is the basis of an economic calculation in which more and more people are involved. No matter whether they invest in a pension fund, on an online platform as a crowd-investor, or directly in a flat as an investment. After all, we are living in a time of responsibilisation, in which we are increasingly being called upon to take market-based personal responsibility in a wide variety of areas of life. In their work &#8220;Zwischen Anlagen Anderer&#8221; (Between the Investments of Others), Michael Meier and Christoph Franz focus on the so-called &#8220;Vorsorgewohnung&#8221; (retirement flat), which is in great demand in the city of Vienna.<br />
  <br />
  In Vienna, too, the question of ownership has an essential influence on the city in its being and becoming. The artists collected contributions from actors and theorists who provide a view of the financialisation of housing from various directions. Michael Meier &#038; Christoph Franz (*1980/*1982) live and work as an artist duo in Zurich.</p>
